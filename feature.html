What We Supply

Mastermine Investment Limited specialises in both wholesale and retail supply of a wide range of products with a strong focus on contract-based services tailored to the specific needs of our corporate clients. We offer comprehensive services in supplying all items across the categories of goods we supply. These categories include but are not limited to ICT equipment, office supplies, stationeries, agricultural input, agricultural output, Construction & engineering material, Electric equipment & components, Office furniture and more.

However, our extensive categories are constantly being adjusted to fit our clients' needs and requirements. Our wholesale services cater to large scale orders, ensuring timely and bulk delivery, while our retail services focus on providing personalized smaller-scale supply to individual customers and businesses. We pride ourselves on maintaining high-quality standards, competitive pricing and seamless logistics to guarantee customer specification in fullfilling both long-term purchase contracts as well as short-term purchase contracts. Mastermine Investment Limited uarantees the best service and that every order is fullfilled in the utmost presicion in the most corporate-oriented system possible.
